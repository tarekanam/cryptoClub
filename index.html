What is The CUNY Crypto Club?

The CUNY Crypto Club at Baruch College is a student-run organization that aims to serve as a central resource for education and emerging trends about Bitcoin, Blockchain Technology, and Cryptocurrencies.

Next

Mission

Our goal is to stablish a space for Blockchain and Cryptocurrency education and research at Baruch College and CUNY affiliate Colleges. Provide students with the tools to become fluent in blockchain technology and distributed application development though speakers, educational material, workshops, challenges, and real world/ hands on projects, and potential job/internship opportunities.

Blockchain Technology, creates a secure, immutable ledger, that takes out errors, middlemen, and fraud from complex systems efficiently. Any complex organizational system / industry is likely to adopt elements of Blockchain in the upcoming decades. Walmart, Government, Real Estate, Healthcare, Supply Chain.
